About Our Company

Established in 1993, Durgesh Security Services Pvt. Ltd. is one of the most trusted security providers in Muzaffarpur, Bihar.

With over three decades of experience, we have built a reputation for providing trained, verified, and disciplined security personnel for residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Our team consists of professionally trained guards who prioritize safety, vigilance, and customer satisfaction.

We are committed to delivering reliable, professional, and affordable security solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.
